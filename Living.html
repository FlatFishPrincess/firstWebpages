Vancouver Culture

When you eat out, you should tip for the service. As some of you are from no-tip culture, this might not be familiar with it. But as you are in Vancouver, follow the Vancouver culture! Usually 15% is good but if you are not happy with service, 10% or less is fine. On the other hand, if you are satisfied with the service the restaurant provide, then you can tip more or 20%. This depends on how you felt the service is. Just keep in mind, Tip is a good way to express your opinion of the restaurant.
