Well-organized international offices in school

As Vancouver is one of the most famous cities for international students, many education institutions offer great quality of service for them. There are some schools that have international offices, admission offices.

According to QS, a large global rankings index on the top universities for student living standards, Vancouver is a major city for students in international education wanting to learn English. In their “Best Student Cities for 2016” profile, they rate Vancouver as being in the top six in terms of students and overall desirability. 

University transfer system

You might hear about UBC(university of British Columbia) or SFU(Simon Fraser University). They are some good examples of Vancouver universities which are well-known for great quality of education. In British Columbia, you can easily transfer to these universities when you meet some extent of GPS in colleges in Vancouver. This is a good opportunity to explore your academic path!

Variety of outdoor activities

Ok, in terms of education, Vancouver is such a great city. What about activities? We don’t always study, but sometimes enjoy going out. That is why Vancouver is such a perfect city for you! As Vancouver is mountainous, you can go hiking anywhere and enjoy skiing or snow walking. Additionally, there are several national parks so that you can see spectacular nature.

Study permit

If you choose your school, you have to be eligible for studying in Vancouver by getting a student permit. It typically takes 2–3 business week to get a Canadian visitor visa.

It is a two step process: Apply online on CIC website by filling up forms, uploading supporting documents and paying the fees. Within next 1-3 weeks, CIC will evaluate your application details and make a decision. Although you can check online how long it is taking on average to process study permits, it is always a good idea to apply as early as possible, and at least four to six months prior to the start of classes. You can apply for a Canadian study permit either online or through a paper application, which can be obtained from the Citizenship and Immigration Canada(CIC) website. Paper applications typically take about twice as long, and it's always recommended to check the processing times well in advance. When you arrive in Canada, a Government of Canada officer will meet you. The officer will ask to see your passport or travel documents.Make sure that you have them with you. Even if you don’t need an Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA) or a visa to enter Canada, the officer will ask you a few questions. They will make sure you meet the requirements to enter Canada. You will also have to show the officer that you will leave Canada at the end of your stay.
For more information, visit CIC website
